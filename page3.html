About Nida

Nida is a true hidden gem of Lithuania, located on the Curonian Spit, a narrow piece of land perched between the Baltic Sea and the Curonian Lagoon.
It's a resort town in Lithuania, famous for one of the tallest dunes in Europe and colourful fishermen houses. Nida is loved by local families choosing this town for summer holidays, but its not well known by the international tourists, therefore its a perfect location for travelers looking for a picturesque and quiet place to relax in the summer.

Best ways to make the most of your stay in Nida

Climb one of the tallest dunes in Europe
Enjoy some forest bathing on your way to the beach
Soak up some sun at the beach which stretches across entire Curonian Spit
Climb hill of the witches
Go on a bike ride to neighboring fishermen villages
